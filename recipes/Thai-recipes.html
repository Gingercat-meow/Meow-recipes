Thai Dipping Sauce

A flavourful Thai chilli lime sauce

Thai Nam Jim or Thai dipping sauce is crazy flavourful and dead easy. Garlic, shallots, lime juice and hot chilies. It’s a Thai wonder sauce. Thai dipping sauce is great on just about everything. Maybe not ice cream but other than that. It’s called nam jim.

    Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice -fresh squeezed
  • 2 green chilies -seeded and minced
  • 1 red chili -seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup shallots minced
  • 1 large clove garlic – as finely chopped as you can
  • 1/4 cup minced cilantro
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Instruction

  1. Combine ingredients. Stir.

  2. Let sit for an hour or more to let the flavours combine.
